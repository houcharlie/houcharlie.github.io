I am an AI research scientist at Meta. I work on topics including (1) developing LLM reasoning models for trust and safety enforcement, and (2) improving the efficiency and quality of text extraction models (OCR/ASR). This work has led to robust improvements in key business metrics like time spent on Instagram.

Prior to working at Meta, I obtained my Ph.D. from CMU advised by Prof. Giulia Fanti. There I led the development of POPri, a step-change improvement in synthetic data quality generation under privacy constraints. I also co-led SquirRL which pioneered the use of reinforcement learning (RL) for identifying security weaknesses in blockchain protocols.

Before my Ph.D., I was an undergrad at Princeton University.
